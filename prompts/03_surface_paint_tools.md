# 03 — Surface Paint Tools

## Goal
Add a color-based paint layer for terrain, water, and broad feature painting.

## Requirements
- Add a paintable surface layer separate from the heightmap layer.
- Support painting at minimum:
  - water
  - land color/terrain color
  - at least one additional feature color category
- Add color/tool selection UI.
- Respect the concept of active layer and active tool.
- Allow toggling visibility of layers.

## Deliverables
- Functional surface paint layer
- Layer visibility controls
- Paint palette/tool selection
- Clear separation between height editing and surface painting

## Notes
- Keep it simple and broad.
- Precise biome logic is not needed yet.
